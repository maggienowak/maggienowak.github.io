Maggie Nowak is a writer who loves fashion, food, travel, and anywhere she can wear a pair of Mickey Mouse ears.

Originally from San Diego, California, she currently lives in New York City. Maggie is set to graduate from Eugene Lang College in May 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in journalism and a minor in screenwriting. You can usually find her baking in her pint-sized East Village kitchen or vintage shopping in Brooklyn.
